# 📊 Full Marketing Plan — Shiv Kathiawadi Thali

> *Developed by MetroMate Real Marketing*

---

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the complete marketing strategy deployed for the brand development of **Shiv Kathiawadi Thali**. The strategy covers offline activation, digital presence building, content marketing, community engagement, and QR-based offline-to-online conversion.

---

## 1. Situation Analysis

### 1.1 Business Background
- Local Kathiawadi cuisine restaurant in Gujarat
- No prior brand identity or digital presence
- Strong word-of-mouth reputation in immediate locality
- Family-owned, authentic recipes

### 1.2 Target Audience

| Segment | Description | Channel |
|---------|------------|--------|
| Local Families | Daily thali eaters, budget-conscious | Pamphlets, WhatsApp |
| Working Professionals | Lunch seekers, office goers | Facebook, Online |
| Students | Value meals, group dining | WhatsApp, Facebook |
| Food Enthusiasts | Authentic regional cuisine lovers | Facebook, Instagram |
| NRI / Diaspora | Nostalgia for Gujarati food | Facebook Page |

### 1.3 Competitive Landscape
- Multiple local dhabas and thali restaurants
- Few with any digital presence
- Opportunity: First-mover digital advantage in hyperlocal market

## 2. Marketing Objectives

1. **Brand Awareness** — Establish Shiv Kathiawadi Thali as the #1 recognized thali brand in the locality
2. **Digital Presence** — Build an active, growing Facebook community of 500+ followers in 90 days
3. **Footfall Increase** — Drive 20% increase in new customer visits via offline + online combined
4. **Reputation Building** — Collect 20+ positive Facebook reviews in first 60 days
5. **Brand Loyalty** — Create repeat customer base via WhatsApp marketing

## 4. Online Marketing Plan

### 4.1 Facebook Marketing

**Page Setup Checklist:**
- [x] Page created with brand name and logo
- [x] Cover photo with brand visual
- [x] About section fully populated
- [x] Location and business hours set
- [x] CTA button (Call Now / Message)
- [x] Directory Links activated
- [x] First 10 posts published
- [x] Page shared to local community groups

**Content Mix (80/20 Rule):**
- 80% Value Content: food photos, recipes, culture, customer stories
- 20% Promotional: offers, discounts, events

**Post Frequency:** 5x/week minimum

### 4.2 WhatsApp Business Marketing

- Business profile setup with catalogue
- Broadcast list for daily specials
- Status updates for thali-of-the-day
- QR code on all materials linking to WhatsApp

### 4.3 QR Code Strategy

QR Code placements:
1. Dining table standees
2. Takeaway packaging
3. Printed menus
4. Entry banner
5. Bill receipts
6. Business cards

Each QR code links directly to the Facebook page for instant following.

## 5. Offline Marketing Plan

### 5.1 Print Materials

| Material | Size | Quantity | Distribution Zone |
|----------|------|----------|-------------------|
| Flex Banner (Main) | 6x4 ft | 2 | Restaurant frontage |
| Flex Banner (Road) | 10x3 ft | 1 | Main road facing |
| Pamphlets | A5 | 1000 | 500m radius |
| Menu Cards | A4 bi-fold | 50 | Tables + counter |
| Table Mats | A3 | 30 | Dining tables |
| Business Cards | Standard | 500 | Counter, handouts |
| QR Standees | A4 frame | 3 | Entry, counter, tables |
| Takeaway Bags | Printed | Bulk | All takeaway orders |

### 5.2 Area Activation

**Zone 1 — Immediate Locality (0-500m)**
- Every household pamphlet drop
- Poster in local grocery shops
- Distribution at nearby temple

**Zone 2 — Extended Locality (500m-2km)**
- Office building distribution (lunch focus)
- Auto-rickshaw stand distribution
- Market area pamphlets

**Zone 3 — Community Events**
- Presence at local Garba / Navratri events
- Distribution during festivals
- Sponsorship of local puja events
