# 💰 Paid Advertising Strategy

## Budget Allocation

| Platform | Monthly Budget | Goal | Expected ROAS |
|---|---|---|---|
| Google Ads (Local) | ₹5,000 | Drive foot traffic | 4x |
| Meta Ads (FB+IG) | ₹4,000 | Brand awareness + orders | 3x |
| Total | ₹9,000 | — | — |

## Google Ads Strategy

### Campaign 1: Local Search Ads
- **Target keywords**: "thali near me", "Kathiawadi food Surat"
- **Ad format**: Responsive Search Ads
- **Bid strategy**: Target CPA
- **Radius**: 5km around restaurant

### Campaign 2: Google Maps Ads
- Enable location extensions
- Promote "Get Directions" CTA
- Show during peak meal hours (12–2 PM, 7–9 PM)

## Meta Ads Strategy

### Campaign 1: Awareness (Reach Objective)
- **Audience**: Surat, 22–45, food interests
- **Creative**: High-quality thali video + "Order Now" CTA
- **Budget**: ₹1,500/month

### Campaign 2: Conversion (Traffic Objective)
- **Audience**: Lookalike from page engagers
- **Creative**: Offer/combo carousel ads
- **Budget**: ₹2,500/month

## Ad Creative Guidelines

- Always show the full thali — abundance sells
- Use warm, vibrant colors
- Include price anchor: "Full Thali @ ₹X only"
- Video ads: 15–30 seconds, first 3 seconds must hook
- Always have a clear CTA: "Order Now" / "Visit Today"

## A/B Testing Plan

| Variable | Version A | Version B |
|---|---|---|
| Headline | "Authentic Kathiawadi Thali" | "Full Thali ₹X Only" |
| CTA | "Order Now" | "Get Directions" |
| Visual | Static photo | Short video reel |
| Audience | Broad interest | Lookalike |
