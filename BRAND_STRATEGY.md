# 📊 Brand Strategy — Shiv Kathiawadi Thali

## Brand Identity

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Brand Name** | Shiv Kathiawadi Thali |
| **Category** | Traditional Gujarati / Kathiawadi Cuisine |
| **Target Market** | Surat, Gujarat & Diaspora |
| **Brand Voice** | Authentic, Warm, Homely |
| **USP** | Authentic Kathiawadi flavors with generous portions |

## Brand Pillars

1. **Authenticity** — Traditional recipes, regional ingredients
2. **Value** — Generous thali, affordable price
3. **Community** — Rooted in Kathiawadi culture
4. **Quality** — Fresh, home-style preparation daily

## Target Audience

- **Primary**: Working professionals aged 22–45 in Surat
- **Secondary**: Gujarati diaspora craving home-style food
- **Tertiary**: Food explorers and thali enthusiasts

## Brand Colors & Tone

| Color | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Saffron / Orange | Energy, warmth, Indian heritage |
| Deep Navy | Trust, professionalism |
| White | Cleanliness, freshness |

## Competitive Positioning

> *"Where every meal feels like home."*

- Traditional over trendy
- Quantity meets quality
- Community-centric marketing
