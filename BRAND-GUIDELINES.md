# 🎨 Brand Guidelines — Shiv Kathiawadi Thali

> *Maintained by MetroMate Real Marketing*

---

## Brand Identity Core

### Brand Name
**Shiv Kathiawadi Thali**
- Short form: **Shiv Thali**
- Never abbreviate to initials

### Tagline
> **"Taste the Tradition"**

### Brand Personality
| Trait | Description |
|-------|------------|
| Warm | Welcoming, homely, family-friendly |
| Proud | Celebrates Kathiawadi heritage |
| Authentic | Real recipes, real stories |
| Accessible | For everyone, every day |

---

## Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Saffron Orange | `#FF6B35` | Primary brand color, CTAs |
| Deep Forest Green | `#1B4332` | Secondary, backgrounds |
| Warm Cream | `#FFF8E7` | Backgrounds, card fills |
| Rich Brown | `#5C3317` | Text accents, borders |
| Golden Yellow | `#FFD700` | Highlights, stars, accents |

---

## Typography

- **Headlines:** Bold Devanagari-style or Display serif fonts
- **Body Copy:** Clean, readable sans-serif (Hindi + English bilingual)
- **Minimum font size:** 12px digital / 10pt print

---

## Logo Usage Rules

- Always maintain clear space around the logo (minimum 20px)
- Never stretch or distort
- Never place on busy backgrounds without overlay
- QR codes must always include the Facebook logo watermark (as deployed)

---

## Voice & Tone

### Do:
- Speak like a proud Gujarati host
- Use warm, inviting language
- Reference local culture and festivals

### Don't:
- Use corporate jargon
- Over-promise on spice levels
- Ignore bilingual (Hindi/Gujarati + English) audiences
