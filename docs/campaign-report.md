## Learnings & Recommendations

### What Worked Well
- QR-to-Facebook conversion at dining tables showed strong engagement
- Festival-themed content performed above average
- Pamphlet distribution in 0-500m zone drove immediate walk-ins

### Recommendations for Phase 2
1. Launch Instagram page with reels (food content performs extremely well)
2. Run Facebook Lead Ads for lunch delivery orders
3. List on Zomato/Swiggy with optimized menu photos
4. Start Google Business Profile for search discoverability
5. Create a WhatsApp loyalty group for repeat customers
