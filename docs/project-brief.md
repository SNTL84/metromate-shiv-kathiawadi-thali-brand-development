## Project Scope

This project covers the **complete brand development** of Shiv Kathiawadi Thali — a local Gujarati restaurant serving authentic Kathiawadi cuisine.

### In Scope
- Brand identity (logo, color palette, typography, tagline)
- Menu design (print + digital)
- Offline marketing collateral (banners, pamphlets, business cards, bags, table mats)
- Facebook Business Page setup and activation
- QR code creation and deployment strategy
- WhatsApp Business setup
- Content calendar (first 30 days)
- Initial social media post creation (first 10 posts)
- Local area marketing execution guidance

### Out of Scope (Phase 2 Potential)
- Paid advertising campaigns
- Instagram presence
- Website / landing page
- Zomato / Swiggy listing optimization
- SEO content marketing
