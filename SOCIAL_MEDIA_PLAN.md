# 📣 Social Media Content Plan

## Platform Strategy

| Platform | Goal | Content Type | Posting Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|
| Instagram | Brand awareness & engagement | Reels, Stories, Posts | 5x/week |
| Facebook | Community building & local reach | Posts, Events, Groups | 3x/week |
| YouTube | Brand storytelling | Shorts, Behind-the-scenes | 2x/week |
| Google Business | Local discovery | Updates, Photos, Reviews | 3x/week |
| LinkedIn | B2B & press mentions | Articles, Milestones | 1x/week |

## Content Pillars (80/20 Rule)

### 80% Value Content
- **Food photography** — vibrant thali shots, ingredients, preparation
- **Behind the scenes** — kitchen prep, fresh ingredients sourcing
- **Cultural stories** — Kathiawadi traditions, festivals, heritage
- **Customer features** — testimonials, user-generated content

### 20% Promotional Content
- Offers and combos
- New item launches
- Events and specials

## Instagram Reel Ideas

1. "Day in the life of our thali kitchen" (BTS)
2. "What's in a Kathiawadi Thali?" (ingredient showcase)
3. "Reaction videos" from first-time customers
4. "Thali ASMR" — plating sounds and colors
5. Festival special announcements
