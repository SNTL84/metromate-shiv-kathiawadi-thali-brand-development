## 🌟 Project Overview

> **"A great brand is not built overnight — it's built one story, one thali, one customer at a time."**

This repository documents the **end-to-end brand development journey** of **Shiv Kathiawadi Thali**, a local Kathiawadi cuisine restaurant in Gujarat, India — conceptualized, built, and launched by **MetroMate Real Marketing**.

The project covers **identity creation**, **offline presence**, **digital activation**, **Facebook presence**, **QR code strategy**, and **customer journey mapping** — making Shiv Kathiawadi Thali a recognizable and trusted food brand in its locality and beyond.

## 👥 About the Client

### 🍲 Shiv Kathiawadi Thali

**Shiv Kathiawadi Thali** serves authentic Kathiawadi cuisine — a regional style of Indian cooking from the Kathiawar peninsula of Gujarat known for its bold, spicy, rustic flavours.

**What makes them special:**
- 🌶️ Authentic regional Kathiawadi recipes passed down generations
- 🍱 Full thali experience — wholesome, value-for-money meals
- 🤝 Family-run with deep community roots
- 🏠 Known for homestyle taste in a restaurant setting

Before MetroMate's intervention, the business operated entirely through **word-of-mouth** with no digital presence, no brand identity, and no marketing strategy.

## 🖥️ Online Marketing Execution

### 📘 Facebook Page Setup & Activation

> 🔗 **[Shiv Kathiawadi Thali — Official Facebook Page](https://www.facebook.com/profile.php?id=61590091033930)**

MetroMate built and activated the complete Facebook presence from scratch:

- ✅ Profile setup with brand logo, cover photo, and description
- ✅ Business Category: Restaurant / Food & Beverage
- ✅ Location, hours, contact details populated
- ✅ Facebook Directory & Links integration
- ✅ Call-to-Action (CTA) button configured
- ✅ Initial content calendar executed (posts, stories, reels)
- ✅ Community engagement strategy deployed

### 📲 QR Code Marketing System

A custom **Shiv Thali Facebook QR Code** was created and deployed:

```
📷 QR CODE STRATEGY

  [Scan QR] ──▶ Facebook Page ──▶ Follow/Like ──▶ Regular Updates
       │
       ├── Placed on dining tables
       ├── Printed on takeaway bags
       ├── Added to menus
       ├── Featured on banners
       └── Shared on WhatsApp
```

*The QR code (included in this repo) bridges offline foot traffic directly to the online Facebook community.*

### 📊 Content Strategy

| Content Type | Frequency | Platform | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| Food Photos | 3x/week | Facebook, WhatsApp | Appetite appeal |
| Thali of the Day | Daily | WhatsApp Status | Daily engagement |
| Festival Specials | Occasion-based | Facebook | Community connect |
| Customer Reviews | Weekly | Facebook | Social proof |
| Behind the Scenes | Bi-weekly | Facebook Reels | Authenticity |
| Offers & Discounts | Monthly | All platforms | Footfall drive |

### 🔍 Local SEO & Discoverability

- Facebook Business listing optimized for local search
- Category tags: Kathiawadi, Thali, Gujarat, Food, Restaurant
- Location-based targeting for sponsored content
- WhatsApp Business profile setup
- Google Maps integration guidance provided

## 📣 Offline Marketing Execution

### 🖨️ Print & Physical Marketing

| Material | Quantity | Placement | Impact |
|----------|----------|-----------|--------|
| **Flex Banners** | Multiple sizes | Entry, road-facing, wall | High visibility |

## 🏆 Why This Project Stands Out

<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%">

### 🎯 What Makes This Brand Development Unique

1. **Zero-to-Brand Journey** — Built from absolute scratch with no prior brand assets
2. **Cultural Authenticity** — Brand identity rooted in Kathiawadi heritage, not generic food brand templates
3. **Offline-First Thinking** — QR code strategy that bridges the physical restaurant to digital community
4. **Budget-Smart Execution** — Maximum impact marketing optimized for a local SME budget
5. **Holistic Approach** — Every touchpoint designed: from table mats to Facebook posts

</td>
<td width="50%">

### 💡 Key Learnings for Food Brand Development

- 🍽️ Food brands grow fastest when **culture is the content**
- 📱 **QR codes at dining tables** are the most underused marketing tool in local F&B
- 👨‍👩‍👧 Family-run restaurants benefit most from **story-driven brand voices**
- 🗺️ **Hyperlocal offline presence** must precede digital campaigns for credibility
- 📸 **Food photography** is not a luxury — it's the single highest-ROI marketing investment for restaurants

</td>
